Getting Started

It's as easy as 1..2..3..4..5

Step 1. Download

 

 

Bear Traffic Control is provided to you in a simple "no install needed" file. All you have to do is download the correct version for your computer, which you can do by clicking here

Step 2. Launch the game

 

 

To launch the game, simply open the file you downloaded in the previous step.

Step 3. Click Play Demo

 

 

At the moment, BTC is only available in the form of a demo. To launch this and play the game, click "Play Demo"

Step 4. Select your difficulty level

 

 

The difficulty selection screen will explain your characters back story, you should read this if you haven't before. It's also on this screen that you can choose the difficulty level you would like to play at. To select one of these simply click on the relevant button.
