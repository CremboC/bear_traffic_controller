The big question. Why Bear Traffic Control?

You may be wondering why we called it Bear Traffic Control and there is a simple answer. Everyone likes bears. They just make life better. Probably.

The goal of BTC. It's really pretty simple.

As a player, all you have to do is get planes through your airspace from their origin to their destination. Just make sure you adhere to separation rules and avoid crashes or your secret will be revealed. "What secret?" you say. You'll have to play to find out.

Built to be expandable. Why limit yourself?

The current version of Bear Traffic Control has been built to fullfill some basic requirements. However, we have designed everything from our classes to our GUI to be as expandable as possible. All of our code is available on GitHub, just search “bear traffic control” or click the button below to be taken straight there.
